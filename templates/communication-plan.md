## Escalation Protocol

| Situation | Escalation Path | Timeline |
|-----------|----------------|----------|
| Technical blocker | Team lead → Engineering manager | Within 24 hours |
| Scope change | PM → Executive sponsor | Within 48 hours |
| Security concern | Any engineer → Security team lead | Immediately |
| Customer-facing outage | On-call → PM → Comms team | Within 15 minutes |
| Timeline slip > 1 week | PM + Tech lead → Steering committee | Before next milestone |
