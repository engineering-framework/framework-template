## Likelihood & Impact Scale

| Score | Likelihood | Impact |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| 1 | Very unlikely (< 10%) | Negligible — minor delay or inconvenience |
| 2 | Unlikely (10–25%) | Minor — small scope or timeline impact |
| 3 | Possible (25–50%) | Moderate — significant rework or delay |
| 4 | Likely (50–75%) | Major — threatens milestone or scope |
| 5 | Very likely (> 75%) | Critical — project failure or major incident |

**Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact**
- 1–4: Low — monitor
- 5–9: Medium — mitigation plan required
- 10–15: High — active mitigation underway; escalate if needed
- 16–25: Critical — immediate action required; escalate to leadership
